Kabhi kabhi lagta hai jaise sab kuch shuru hi us shaam se hua tha… ya shayad usse bhi pehle… jab koi naam nahi tha, koi roop nahi tha, bas ek halka sa ehsaas tha… jaise hawa kisi anjaan raaste se guzarti hui achanak se dil ko chhoo jaaye aur bina rukke aage nikal jaaye… aur tu… tu wahi ehsaas ban kar aayi thi… bina poore hue, bina samjhe hue, phir bhi poori…

Main aaj bhi samajhne ki koshish karta hoon ki tu kya thi… par har baar sawal khud hi khud mein gir jaata hai… jaise paani mein pathar phenko aur lehron ke baad phir wahi shaant satah reh jaaye… tu sawal bhi thi aur jawab bhi… aur shayad isi liye main kabhi tujhe pakad nahi paaya… kyunki tujhe pakadna matlab us behte hue pal ko rokna… aur jo beh raha ho, use kaise roka jaata hai…

Tu saath nahi thi… phir bhi har jagah thi… subah ki pehli soch mein, raat ke aakhiri sawal mein… un khaali palon mein jahan aadmi khud se milta hai… aur jab milta hai, to thoda darr jaata hai… kyunki wahan koi aur nahi hota… sirf sach hota hai… aur us sach mein bhi tu thi… ya shayad tu hi wo sach thi…

Kabhi lagta tha tu pyaar hai… phir lagta tha nahi, ye kuch aur hai… pyaar se bhi halka… pyaar se bhi gehra… jaise kisi ne dil ke andar ek khidki khol di ho aur wahan se poora aasmaan dikhne lage… aur aadmi confuse ho jaaye ki wo khidki dekh raha hai ya aasmaan…

Main tere paas aana chahta tha… par phir lagta tha ki main already tere paas hoon… ya shayad tere andar… ya shayad tu mere andar… ye jo boundaries hoti hain na, “main” aur “tu” ki… ye dheere dheere ghulne lagti thi… jaise barish ke baad mitti aur paani alag nahi pehchaane jaate… bas khushboo reh jaati hai… aur wahi khushboo tu thi…

Aur phir ek ajeeb si baat samajh aane lagi… ki shayad tu kabhi thi hi nahi… ya shayad tu hamesha thi… bas main pehli baar dekh paaya… pehli baar mehsoos kar paaya… pehli baar apne aap ko thoda side rakh kar kuch aur ban paaya… kuch halka… kuch bachcha jaisa… bina wajah muskuraane wala… bina wajah ro dene wala…

Wahi bachcha jo taaron ko dekh kar khush ho jaata hai… jo mitti ki khushboo mein ghar dhoond leta hai… jo hawa ko pakadne ki koshish karta hai… aur pakad nahi paata, phir bhi hasta rehta hai… tu ne mujhe wahi bana diya… ya shayad main pehle se tha… bas bhool gaya tha…

Kabhi kabhi lagta hai ki main tujhmein kho jaana chahta hoon… par phir ek aur awaaz aati hai… ke kho jaana bhi kya hota hai… jab do cheezein alag ho tabhi to koi kisi mein kho sakta hai… agar pehle se hi ek ho, to kho jaana sirf ek bhram hai… aur shayad wahi bhram sabse sundar hota hai…

Tu bandhan bhi thi… aur azaadi bhi… tu ne baandha bhi… aur chhoda bhi… aur iss khel mein hi kuch toot gaya… ya shayad kuch ban gaya… kuch aisa jo pehle kabhi tha hi nahi… ek halka sa shunya… jahan darr nahi tha… jahan maut ka bhi koi matlab nahi tha… jahan bas hona tha… aur us hone mein ek ajeeb si shanti thi…

Main sochta hoon agar tu saamne hoti… to kya main itna hi mehsoos kar pata… ya ye sab sirf isliye hai kyunki tu door hai… kyunki tu poori nahi hui… kyunki tu ek khwaab bani rahi… aur khwaab… khwaab sach se zyada sacche lagte hain… kyunki wahan koi seema nahi hoti… koi samajh nahi hota… koi darr nahi hota… bas ek behta hua anubhav hota hai…

Aur shayad isi liye dil raat ko chhat par jaata hai… taaron ko dekhta hai… gaane sunta hai… aur khud se poochta hai… kyun… kyun itna mehsoos hota hai… kyun itni gehraai hai is sab mein… kyun ek anjaan sa ehsaas itna apna lagta hai…

Koi jawab nahi aata… bas aankhon mein halka sa paani aa jaata hai… aur dil thoda halka ho jaata hai… jaise kisi ne andar jama hua sab kuch dheere se chhoo kar keh diya ho… “theek hai… sab theek hai…”

Tu thi ya nahi thi… iska shayad kabhi jawab na mile… par jo hua… jo beh gaya… jo mehsoos hua… wo kahin na kahin ab bhi chal raha hai… ek nadi ki tarah… chup chaap… bina shor ke…

Aur main… main bas us kinare baitha hoon… haath paani mein daale hue… kabhi lehron ko mehsoos karta hua… kabhi bas dekhte hue…

Aur dheere dheere… pata bhi nahi chalta… ki kab main kinara reh jaata hoon… aur kab nadi ban jaata hoon…
