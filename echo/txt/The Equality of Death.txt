Life aur death nature ka rule hai. Saare living beings equal hain. Sabko jeene ka right hai, aur marne ka bhi. Koi royal ho ya koi lower being, maut ke saamne insaan aur suar mein kya farq? Dono marne ke baad bas dead hi hain.

Aise challenge mein agar kisi se kuchalwana nahi hai, to do tareeke hain — ya to itne strong ban jao ki koi tumhe dabaane ki soch bhi na sake, ya phir aise ban jao ki koi tumhare paas aana bhi na chahe.

Insaan alag-alag islands jaise hote hain, jo fate ke sea mein float kar rahe hote hain. Logon ka milna bas in lonely islands ke takraane jaisa hai. Ek baar takra gaye to kuch effect to hoga hi. Kabhi ye interest, rishton, dosti, pyaar ya nafrat ke naam pe saath jud jaate hain. Par aakhir mein alag ho hi jaate hain, aur dheere dheere destruction ki taraf chal padte hain.

Kabhi main cheekha tha, phir dheere dheere meri awaaz hi chali gayi.

Kabhi main roya tha, phir dheere dheere mere aansu hi khatam ho gaye.

Kabhi main dukh mein toota tha, phir dheere dheere main har cheez sehna seekh gaya.

Kabhi main khush bhi hua tha, phir dheere dheere duniya se farq padna band ho gaya.
