**Love Is Wind — Flow, Freedom & The Courage To Be Alive**

Nothing is permanent.
Aur shayad isi wajah se zindagi khoobsurat lagti hai.

Agar phool hamesha khila rahe, to phool hone ka koi arth hi nahi bachega.
Agar subah kabhi shaam mein na badle, to roshni ki keemat kaun samjhega?
Waqt ka matlab hi badlav hai.
Change ke bina time sirf ek idea hota — jeevan nahi.

Time exist hi isliye karta hai kyunki kuch bhi thehra hua nahi hai.
Sab kuch beh raha hai.
Agar poora brahmaand achanak ek static tasveer ban jaaye,
to time bhi apna matlab kho dega.
Tasveer mein waqt nahi hota.
Waqt ki zarurat sirf isi liye padi, kyunki cheezein badal rahi hain.
Time ek ghadi nahi — ek response hai change ka.

Osho kehte hain,
“Life is not a noun, it is a verb.”

Zindagi koi cheez nahi jo rakh lo, pakad lo, secure kar lo.
Zindagi ek ghatna hai — jo ho rahi hai, jo beh rahi hai.
Aur jo beh raha hai, wahi zinda hai.
Wahi sundar hai.

Isliye static beauty asal mein beauty hoti hi nahi.
Wo sirf ek murda frame hoti hai.

Ek bilkul shaant aasmaan ke beech jab ek panchi udta hai,
to nazar sirf panchi par kyun tik jaati hai?
Kyunki aasmaan nahi, panchi jeevit hai.

Baarish ki boondon mein jaadu nahi hota.
Jaadu unke girne mein hota hai.
Kohra isliye khoobsurat lagta hai kyunki wo rukta nahi —
aata hai, chhoota hai, aur chala jaata hai.
Nature kahin bhi thehar kar explanation nahi deti.
Wo bas hoti hai.

Rumi likhte hain:
“Why are you so busy with this or that, good or bad?
Pay attention to how things blend.”

Life koi maths problem nahi hai jo solve ho jaaye.
Life poetry hai.
Experience hai.
Mistery hai.

Phir bhi insaan ko rukna sikhaya jaata hai.

Poora brahmaand ghoom raha hai.
Atoms vibrate kar rahe hain.
Stars paida ho rahe hain, mit rahe hain.
Phool khilta hai, murjhata hai.
Nadi behti hai.
Baarish ki boondein girkar turant khatam ho jaati hain —
aur unhe girne ka koi afsos nahi hota.
Warna kaun itni unchai tak jaakar girne ki talaash karta?

Upar tikne mein maza nahi hai.
Neeche rehne mein bhi nahi.
Maza movement mein hai.
Dynamism hi joy create karta hai.

Lekin insaan ko kaha jaata hai:
ek jagah tik jao,
ek pehchaan bana lo,
ek role pakad lo,
settle ho jao,
ek hi insaan ke ho jao,
aur phir usi mein mar jao.

Phool ko kamre mein band kar do,
na hawa use chhoo sake,
na baarish usse baat kar sake —
to phool ke paas marne ke alawa koi rasta nahi bachta.

usi ko galat saabit karne mein laga hai.

ego is a seed, a protective seed that protects us from light, water, and all that is necessary for our freedom, mukti, moksh, enlightenment, kuch bhi keh lo
it is a protective trap, a cozy prison for a lazy bird

usi ke khilaaf khada ho jaana —
sirf isliye kyunki wo control nahi kar pa raha?

Krishnamurti kehte hain:
“Security is an illusion. Life is movement.”

Lekin hum movement se darte hain.
Kyunki movement ka matlab hai unknown.
unknown mein jaana andhere mein jaane jaisa hai na, isliye darr lagta hai
kya hoga kuch kaha nahi ja sakta...
Aur unknown mein ego zinda nahi reh sakta.
seed zinda nahi rahega, lekin ped shayad ug jaaye...

Future unknown hai —
aur isi wajah se khoobsurat hai.
Ek suspense, ek rahasya, ek khula hua sawaal.

Socho agar ek din future bhi table ki tarah define ho jaaye.
Har insaan ki poori zindagi scripted ho.
Har natural phenomenon predictable ho.
Sab kuch information ban jaaye.

Kya phir jeene ka mann karega?

Jo mil jaaye wo mitti ban jaata hai.
Jo anjaana hai, jismein rahasya hai —
wahi sona hai.

Yahin par hum pyaar ko bhi galat samajh lete hain.

Hum pyaar ko relationship bana dete hain.
Naam, label, planning, future — sab chipka dete hain.

Osho kehte hain:
“Love is a state of being, not a relationship.”

Pyaar tum aur kisi ke beech nahi hota.
Pyaar tumhare andar hota hai.
Tum source ho.

Jaise heat zyada hoti hai to wo thandi jagah ki taraf behne lagti hai,
waise hi jab tumhare andar gratitude, anugraha, jeevan ke prati thankfulness bhar jaati hai,
to pyaar apne aap bahar behne lagta hai.
Energy static nahi reh sakti.

Isi liye Buddha, Mahavir, Jesus logon ko kheench laate the.

sirf meri naak ke liye khushboo banana.

sirf mere bageeche mein behna.

agar tu gaya, main toot jaunga.

“Set your life on fire.”

Lekin hum aag ko dabaa dete hain,
taaki ghar jal na jaaye.

Aur phir poori zindagi raakh ke saath jeete hain.

Sachcha pyaar selective nahi hota.

tum sirf ek insaan se pyaar nahi karte —
tum poori existence se pyaar karte ho.

to attachment ban jaata hai.

“Love dies of routine.”

Stability zindagi nahi hai.

Zindagi mein dhoop bhi hai, chhaanv bhi.
