Poetry ko log aksar galat samajh lete hain.
Unhe lagta hai poetry ka matlab hai khoobsurti, phool, chaand, halki si meethi udaasi.
Par sach itna naram nahi hota.

Poetry sundar nahi hoti — poetry sach hoti hai.
Aur sach ka apna ek temperature hota hai… kabhi thanda, kabhi jalta hua.
Sach aksar chubhta hai, kabhi andar se cheer deta hai, kabhi bas ek khamosh daraar chhod deta hai jise tum din bhar ignore karte ho… par raat ko woh phir se dhadakne lagti hai.

Socho zara — agar insaan ko poori azaadi hoti mehsoos karne ki…
agar bachpan se kisi ne na kaha hota “ro mat”,
agar kisi ne na sikhaya hota “itna mat haso”,
agar kisi ne na samjhaya hota “zyada mat bolo, log kya kahenge”…
to kya poetry aaj is form mein hoti?

Shayad nahi.
Shayad har saans hi kavita hoti.
Shayad chalna bhi ek geet hota.
Shayad insaan ko alag se likhne ki zarurat hi na padti — kyunki jeena hi abhivyakti hota.

Par hum us duniya mein nahi rehte.

Hum ek “civilized” society mein rehte hain — aur is civilization ka sabse refined, sabse polished jhoot ye hai ki tumhe pehle acceptable banna padega, tabhi tumhe jagah milegi.
Yahan seedha rule nahi bola jaata, par har jagah hawa mein likha hota hai:
“Apne aap jaise mat rehna.”

Bolne se pehle socho.
Mehsoos karne se pehle control karo.
Sach kehne se pehle apni image check karo.

Aur dheere dheere pata bhi nahi chalta — par hum jeena nahi, edit karna shuru kar dete hain.
Roz.

Subah uthte hi ek invisible mask pehen lete hain.
Office version alag.
Family version alag.
Social media version alag.
Aur asli jo hai na…
wo beech mein kahin chup kar baith jaata hai.

Phir ek din achanak koi kavita saamne aa jaati hai — bina filter ki, bina permission ki, seedha dil se nikli hui — aur hum hil jaate hain.
Kyun?

Kyuki wo wo bol deti hai jo hum saalon se dabaye baithe hain.
Wo seedha wahan haath rakh deti hai jahan humne khud dekhna band kar diya tha.

Isi liye art disturb karta hai.
Isi liye ek sachcha geet sunke kabhi aankhen bhar aati hain bina reason ke.
Isi liye kabhi kabhi ek painting ko dekh kar ajeeb si bechaini hoti hai.

Andar kuch hilta hai.

Jaise koi purani deewar mein daraar pad gayi ho.
Jaise kisi ne dheere se yaad dilaya ho —
“Tu itna bhi zinda ho sakta tha… par tune compromise kar liya.”

Aur yahin se dard shuru hota hai.

Hum poori zindagi “achha dikhne” ke project mein lage rehte hain.
Image. Reputation. Approval.

Par kabhi ruk ke socha hai?

Tumne apna janm choose nahi kiya.
Tumne apni body choose nahi ki.
Tumne apni family choose nahi ki.
Phir itni mehnat kis image ko bachane mein lag rahi hai?

Kiski nazron mein perfect banna hai?

Society ko categories pasand hain.
Normal.
Abnormal.
Successful.
Pagal.

Jo neatly kisi box mein fit ho jaaye — safe.
Jo thoda sa overflow kare — uncomfortable.
Jo poora hi bahar aa jaaye — khatarnaak.

Isliye history uthao to ek ajeeb pattern dikhega.
Jo log perfectly normal the… duniya unhe bhool gayi.
Jo log thode se tedhe the, thode se jalte hue, thode se bechain — wahi yaad reh gaye.

Kyuki unmein rehearsal nahi thi.
Unmein polish kam thi, par intensity zyada thi.
Unki aankhon mein jo gehraai hoti hai na…
wo logon ko daraati bhi hai aur kheechti bhi.

Tum unhe ignore nahi kar paate.

Sachchi creativity comfortable nahi hoti.
Wo system-friendly nahi hoti.
Wo permission le kar paida nahi hoti.

Isliye jab koi asli kavita janm leti hai, wo sirf shabd nahi hoti — wo ek chhoti si kranti hoti hai.
Bilkul shant… par andar se visphotak.

Wo chillati nahi, par hila deti hai.
Wo todti nahi, par jhooth ko unstable kar deti hai.

Aur shayad isi liye jab tum ek gehri kavita padhte ho na…
to sirf sukoon nahi milta.
Kuch aur bhi hota hai.

Thoda sa guilt.
Thodi si kasak.
Thoda sa sawaal jo chipak jaata hai:

“Main jee raha hoon…
ya bas manage kar raha hoon?”

Aur agar kabhi — bas kabhi —
kisi kavita ko padh kar tumhein lage ki andar koi purani neend toot rahi hai,
saansen thodi gehri ho gayi hain,
aur duniya wahi hote hue bhi thodi si alag lag rahi hai…

to shayad samajh lena —
poetry ne apna kaam kar diya.

Problem kabhi poetry nahi thi.

Shayad problem ye thi
ki humne zindagi ko bahut chhota jeena accept kar liya.
