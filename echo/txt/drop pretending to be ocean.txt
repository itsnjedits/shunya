## 🌌 **Poem — A Drop and the Ocean**

I tried to become
an ocean of love,
yet I discovered —
I was only an innocent drop
of that very ocean.

I did not love you;
love simply flowed through me,
like a sudden wind
reminding wings to fly.

There was something —
a fire and a silence together,
burning within my heart,
melting life and death
into a single flash.

Some magic moved in the air,
gently dissolving
the distance between me and myself.

You…
perhaps were only a medium
through whom
existence touched me.

God danced through you
and descended into me —
an invisible thread
was woven between our souls.

No claim,
no possession,
only a bridge
between you and me.

Mind stopped for a while
Thoughts evaporated in the air,
Breath deepened…
a strange ecstasy,
a profound relaxation —
as if I were dissolving
into existence itself.

You were
my first glimpse of non-duality.

Sometimes you were
the warmth of earth,
that gave me shelter…
sometimes you were
pure light...
flowing through my soul.

You entered my life
like light,
and left me
with the courage to live.

Yet what was it…
I could never understand.

Neither sorrow,
nor joy,
nor longing…

Only an unknown magic
that shattered all dualities.

My breathing slowed…
time became weightless…

And something within whispered —
this is beyond words.

It was simply
a gift from existence,
a glimpse of God
shown through you.

Time moves on…
yet I still feel fresh.

You were not mine,
nor was I yours…

We both were
messages of the One.

Love was the bridge
that connected us.

Some mysteries
are meant to remain mysteries…

So here,
words come to rest,
the rest that i lived...

to become
an ocean of love.

Existence smiled —
and showed me
I was only
an innocent drop
of that same ocean.

I did not love you.

Love…
simply
passed through me.

Like a mild wind

to the wings —
yet
teaches them
to fly.

Something happened.

Not outside —
within.

A fire…
and a vast silence
met
in the cave of my heart.

Life and death
lost their arguments
in a single
lightning-breath.

The air
was no longer empty.

It was
alive…
breathing…
watching.

And slowly —
very slowly —

the distance
between me
and myself
began to dissolve.

You…

you were never the destination.

Just
a doorway
through which
existence
entered me.

God did not come.

God
happened.

Through you —
He danced.

Through me —
He burned.

And somewhere
between your presence
and my disappearance,

an invisible thread
was born.

Not tying us —
but
melting us.

No claim.
No possession.
No story.

Just a silent bridge
appearing
in the space
where “you” and “I”
forgot themselves.

Then—

mind
paused.

Thoughts
fell
like dry leaves
into nothingness.

Breath…
deep…
slow…
ancient…

And I was no longer
breathing.

Existence

A strange ecstasy.
A dangerous peace.

As if
I was being
lovingly erased.

You were

my first crack
in the wall

Sometimes
you were earth —
warm, patient, holding.

Sometimes
you were light —
merciless, pure, penetrating.

And sometimes…

you were simply
absence

You came
like dawn

You left
like fragrance

Yet something remained.

What was it?

Not sorrow.
Not joy.
Not desire.
Not fulfillment.

Just
a trembling mystery

My breath slowed.

Time…

And in the innermost silence

This was never love

This was existence
recognizing

Through you.

Through me.

For a moment —

Time has moved.

You were not mine.

Some mysteries

Silence
takes over.

And what remains

but

a living flame
without smoke,
a silent thunder
without sound,

still
burning,

still
empty,

still
one.
