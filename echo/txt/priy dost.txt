Tu Ek Chehra Nahi

Tu ek chehra nahi,
ek ehsaas hai.

Teri aankhein aaina nahi,
khud parmatma ka jhalakta hua prashn hain.

Teri muskurahat
koi jawaab nahi —
ek chamatkar hai.

Tera chehra
yaad nahi kar paata main,
kyunki kaun parmatma ko
apne chhote se dil mein
kaid kar paaya hai?

Tu ek ehsaas hai
jo sirf mehsoos hota hai,
aur jo jeevan bhar
mehsoos hota rahega.

Baarish ki boondon mein
teri awaaz chhupi hai,
baadalon mein
tera saaya hai,
aandhiyon mein
teri khushboo hai.

Tu roz chhoo kar jaati hai mujhe,
lekin shayad
tujhe bhi iska ehsaas nahi.

Itni bikhri hui hai tu,
itna bada hai tera saaya,
ki kisi ek chhoti si yaad mein
tu samaa hi nahi paati.

Tujhe yaad karne ke liye
tujhmein bikharna padta hai,
tujhe milne ke liye
khud ko khona padta hai.

Main apne aap ko
aur tujhko
ek saath kabhi nahi dekh paunga—
ya to main rahunga,
ya tu.

Par tujh mein bikharne ka
ek anand hai.

Dil badi-badi baatein sochta hai,
lekin teri ek muskurahat
mujhe phir se bachcha bana deti hai.

Saara gyaan,
saara hisaab-kitaab
ek pal mein gir jaata hai.

Physics aur maths jaanta hoon,
par tere aage
koi equation kaam nahi karti.

Yahaan sirf pyaar behta hai—
jo naap-tol nahi jaanta,
jo baadh bankar behta hai.

Pyaar logic nahi jaanta.

Kai din murda tha main,
zinda hue ek mahina ho gaya hai.

Socha tha
tere liye aansu nahi bahaunga,
par aaj
khud ko rok nahi paaya.

Tu aansu bankar aayi,
aur mujhe
nayi zindagi dekar
chali gayi.

Jab bhi aati hai,
aansu ban kar behti hai,
aur ek nayi saans
tohfe mein chhod jaati hai.

Aansu, saans, vichaar, bhaav—
shayad sab
tera hi roop hain.

Warna kaun
itne roop le kar
itna satata hai?

Tujhe mujhe satane ka
shauk hai na?

Kabhi aansuon se,
kabhi yaadon se,
kabhi khwaabon mein,
kabhi saanson mein.

Tu jitna chahe
sata le mujhe,
main tujhe
kabhi bhool nahi paunga.

Tu hasti bohot hai—
kabhi khush bhi raha kar.

Mujhe likhta dekh,
meri likhi baatein padh kar
kabhi hasegi,
kabhi royegi,
kabhi tang hogi.

Par mera naam sunkar
tere chehre par
ek muskurahat
zaroor aayegi.

Main chahta hoon
tujhe saari khushiyan dena,
warna hisaab
adhaura reh jaayega,
meri priy dost.

Tera naam nahi likhunga,
kyunki tu
ek naam mein samaane wali cheez nahi.

Tu ehsaas hai,
anubhav hai,
baarish ki boond,
sardiyon ki dhoop,
garmiyon ki shaam,
aandhiyon mein behne wali hawa.

Tujhse baat karne ke
kayi tareeke hain—
ped-paudhon se,
boondon se,
ugte sooraj se,
chaand se,
aansuon se,
muskurahat se.

Aaj tujhse baat karni thi—
kar li.
Ab sukoon hai.

Dekha,
maine kaha tha na—
tu yaadon mein aati hai
aur zindagi ban kar
chali jaati hai.

Socha tha kavita likhunga,
par shabd kam pad gaye,
to jo mann mein tha
likh diya.

Shuru badi baaton se kiya tha,
par tera khayal aate hi
phir bachcha ban gaya.

Kaha tha na—
ya to tu hogi,
ya kavita hogi.

Aur jab parmatma ki baat hoti hai,
to kuch shesh nahi rehta,
saari banavat mit jaati hai.

Sirf ek bachcha reh jaata hai…
meri priy dost.
