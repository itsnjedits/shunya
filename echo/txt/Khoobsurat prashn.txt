Kab shuru hua tha ye… yaad nahi. Shayad kisi shaam ki halki si dhun mein… ya us pal mein jab aankhon ne pehli baar kuch dekha nahi, par mehsoos kar liya tha. Tab se ek ajeeb si baat chal rahi hai andar… jaise koi aata hai, chhoo kar nikal jaata hai… aur phir bhi kahin jaata nahi.

Kabhi lagta hai koi hai… bilkul paas… itna paas ki saans lene mein bhi halka sa uska ehsaas ghul jaata hai. Aur kabhi lagta hai… shayad kuch bhi nahi… sirf mann ki hi koi leher thi… jo uth kar baith gayi. Par phir bhi… jo reh jaata hai, usse jhooth nahi kaha jaa sakta. Aankhein aksar sach bol deti hain… bina ijazat.

Ajeeb hai na… chehra dhundla pad jaata hai dheere dheere… par ehsaas aur saaf hota chala jaata hai. Jaise waqt chehre mita deta hai… par sparsh ko gehra karta chala jaata hai. Kabhi kabhi bas itna yaad rehta hai… ki kahin sir rakhne ka mann karta tha… bina kisi wajah ke… aur phir wahan rakh kar sab theek lagne lagta tha… bina theek hue.

Shayad isi ko log alag alag naam dete honge… par naam dene se kuch badalta nahi. Kuch cheezein naam se chhoti ho jaati hain. Aur ye… jo bhi hai… chhota hone se inkaar karta hai.

Kabhi wo dharti jaisa lagta hai… jahan gir jao to bhi chot nahi lagti… balki ek garmahat milti hai… jaise kabhi kho gaya tha aur wapas mil gaya hoon. Aur kabhi wahi roshni ban kar andar tak cheer deta hai… itni seedhi… itni saaf… ki khud se nazar churana mushkil ho jaata hai. Us pal aansu aa jaate hain… aur ajeeb baat ye hai ki unhe samjhane ka mann nahi karta… kyunki kuch cheezein samjhayi nahi jaati… sirf behne di jaati hain.

Kabhi wo itni shaant hoti hai ki darr lagta hai… jaise toofaan aane se pehle ka sannata… aur kabhi khud hi toofaan ban kar sab ulat pulat kar deti hai… par baad mein jo bachta hai na… wo pehle se zyada saaf hota hai. Jaise kisi ne andar jama sab kuch hila kar bas asli cheez chhod di ho.

Kabhi ek khwaab ban kar aati hai… itna saaf, itna komal… ki uthne ka mann hi nahi karta. Wahan sab kuch asaan hota hai… log waise hote hain jaise hone chahiye… koi bojh nahi, koi dar nahi… bas ek halka sa hasna… aur us hansi mein poora jeevan sama jaata hai. Phir aankh khulti hai… aur sab wapas apni jagah par aa jaata hai… par kuch hai jo wapas nahi aata… ya shayad wahi sabse zyada reh jaata hai.

Kabhi lagta hai ye sab bas mann ka khel hai… ek sundar sa jhooth… par phir sochta hoon… agar jhooth itna gehra ho sakta hai, to sach kitna gehra hoga. Shayad dono mein farq utna nahi jitna hum samajhte hain.

Kabhi hawa ki tarah chhoo kar nikal jaata hai… itna halka ki pakadna mumkin nahi… aur kabhi itna gehra ho jaata hai ki lagta hai bas isi mein doob jaun… aur phir wapas aane ki zaroorat hi na pade. Kabhi ek geet ban kar nikalta hai… bina soche… bina likhe… jaise koi aur gaa raha ho andar se. Kabhi ek khamoshi… jo sab kuch keh deti hai.

Aur kabhi bas chhat par baith kar aasmaan dekhte hue… bina kisi wajah ke muskurahat aa jaati hai… ya aankhein bhar aati hain. Koi sawal nahi hota… phir bhi sab sawal wahi khade hote hain. Kyun… kaise… kab… par jawab aane ki jaldi nahi hoti.

Ajeeb si baat hai… azaadi chahte chahte kab kisi thehraav ki zaroorat mehsoos hone lagti hai… pata hi nahi chalta. Aur phir samajh nahi aata ki ye thehraav bandhan hai ya ghar. Shayad dono nahi… ya dono hi hain… ek saath.

Kabhi lagta hai bas ek pal ke liye sab chhod kar kahin door nikal jaun… kisi khule maidaan mein… jahan sirf hawa ho… aasmaan ho… aur wo halka sa ehsaas… jo naam se pare hai. Shayad wahan ja kar samajh aaye… ya shayad aur bhi gum ho jaaye… par kuch to badlega.

Aur phir dheere dheere ek aur baat ubharne lagti hai… ki shayad ye sab kisi aur ke baare mein kabhi tha hi nahi. Shayad wo sirf ek darwaza tha… jisse guzarte hue pehli baar khud se mulaqat hui. Isliye itna apna lagta hai… isliye itna gehra chubh jaata hai… kyunki jo dikha… wo bahar nahi… andar tha.

Tab se ek nadi chal padi hai… bina rukke… bina pooche… kabhi tez… kabhi dheemi… kabhi aansuon ki tarah… kabhi hansne ki tarah… kabhi prarthna ban kar jhuk jaati hai… kabhi khamoshi ban kar khadi rehti hai…

Aur main… kabhi kinara ho jaata hoon… kabhi leher… kabhi bas paani…

Farq dheere dheere ghulta jaa raha hai…

Aur shayad isi ghulne mein… jo bachta hai…

wahi hai.
