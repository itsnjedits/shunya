# **“Tujh Mein Mitne Ka Arth”**

Hamari mulaqaaton ki yaadein
Kuch is qadar toot padi mujh par…
Na chehra tha, na aawaaz,
Bas ek khamosh ehsaas —
Jaise kisi anjaan aasmaan se
Yaadon ka toofaan
Baras pada ho mujh par.

Kabhi sochta hoon
Ye teri yaadein hain…
Ya mera hi koi tukda
Jo tere roop mein
Mujhe laut-laut kar pukarta hai.

Teri yaadon ne mujhe shayar bana diya…
Warna kahan shauk tha mujhe
Is alfaazon ke qafile ka malik banne ka.

Main to bas gumnam rehna chahta tha
Tere dil ki kisi khamosh si gali mein…
Magar na jaane kaise
Ye lafz mere andar
Samundar ban kar ubhar aaye.

Akela hi raha main
Is veerani basti mein…
Shayad tere khwaab hi
Is tanhaayi ki wajah bane.

Tere saath khula aasman dekhna chahta tha,
Magar chaar deewari kamre mein
Ye jawaani mere armaano ki tarha
khamoshi se jalti gayi...

Main tujhe yaad karta hoon,
Ya teri yaadein mujh par
Hamla kar deti hain...

Tab sochta hoon —
Gunahgaar kaun hai?

Main?
Teri yaadein?
Ya wo khel
Jo kismat aur rooh
Mil kar rachti hain?

Tujhe kabhi kagaz par likhta hu
Kabhi hathon ki lakeeron mein dekha karta hu
Koi shaam nahi guzarti is qalam aur syahi ke bina
Bachpan mein kahan itna likhta tha...

Shayad kabhi
Ye likhna-likhaana chhoot bhi jaaye…
Main to anpadh hi khush tha
Tere saaye mein.

Kabhi kabhi mann karta hai
Phir se bachcha ban jaun…
Teri god mein sar rakh kar
Duniya bhool jaun.

Tujh mein kabhi
Maa jaisi karuna ki jhalak milti thi…
Lagta tha jaise
Tere aanchal mein
Ek poora janam guzar sakta hoon.

Magar dheere dheere samjha…
Mujhe tujhe paana nahi tha.

Mujhe to bas
Tujh mein
Mit jaana tha.

Kaash tu sagar hoti…
Aur main ek leher.

Jo janam leti bhi tujh se,
Aur aakhir mein
Tujh mein hi sama jaati.

Kabhi bewajah muskurata hoon,
Kabhi barsaaton mein
Khud se hi lipat kar roya karta hoon.

Teri koi khabar nahi,
Magar chaaron taraf
Tera hi ehsaas hai.

Tab sawal uthta hai —
Ye pyaar hai?
Ya bas akelepan ka jaadu?

Ya phir
Ye rooh ka safar hai
Jo kisi aur rooh ke zariye
Khud ko pehchaan raha hai?

Kabhi khwaab mein dekhta hoon
Hum dono bachchon ki tarah
Khule maidaan mein daud rahe hain…

Phir achanak
Suraj ki pehli kiran aati hai
Aur tujhe mita deti hai.

Main hosh mein aa jaata hoon.

Tab sochta hoon —
Tu haqeeqat thi
Ya bas ek sapna?

Ya phir
Tu ek darpan thi
Jismein main
Apni hi rooh ka chehra dekh raha tha?

Jo pal tere saath guzre
Wo yaadein thi
Ya astitva ki koi gehri saazish?

Maine khud ko kho diya tha…
Ya wahi pehli baar
Khud ko paaya tha?

Ye rooh aakhir
Kyun tadapti hai
Un yaadon mein?

Kyun baar baar
Bikharne ka mann karta hai
Tujh mein?

Kabhi lagta hai
Tu khuda ka tohfa thi.

Kabhi lagta hai
Tu khuda ka hi ek roop thi.

Aur kabhi lagta hai
Tu bas ek zariya thi —
Mujhe
Mujh se milane ka.

Aaj bhi jab
Tera ehsaas hawa ki tarah
Mujh se takraata hai…

Main mitti ban kar
Us hawa mein beh jaata hoon.

Jaise kisi bhooli hui rooh ko
Apna asli ghar
Yaad aa gaya ho.

Shayad tune kabhi
Ye sab mehsoos na kiya ho…

Aur dil se chahta hoon
Ke tu kabhi na kare.

Kyuki shayari
Sirf lafzon ka khel nahi hoti.

Ye jazbaaton ka toofaan hai.

Is toofaan mein
Insaan ya to mazboot ho jaata hai…
Ya phir
Bilkul toot jaata hai.

Aur sach kahun —
Aaj tak samajh nahi paaya
Main mazboot bana hoon
Ya toot gaya hoon.

Magar itna zaroor samjha hoon…

Ye kavita
Na sahi hai
Na galat.

Ye bas ek nritya hai —
Mann ka, rooh ka, shabdon ka.

Jise likh dene se
Dil mein ek thehraav sa aa jaata hai.

Jaise aakash se badal hat jaate hain…

Jaise leher
Aakhir sagar mein
Laut aati hai.

Aur tab samajh aata hai —

Leher kabhi alag thi hi nahi.

Wo hamesha se
Sagar hi thi.

### **Legendary Ending (Spiritual Closure)**

Magar ab dheere dheere samajh aane laga hai…

Shayad tu meri manzil nahi thi.
Shayad tu bas ek raasta thi.

Ek darpan…
Jismein dekh kar
Maine pehli baar
Apni rooh ka chehra pehchana.

Main tujhe dhoondta raha
Har yaad, har khwaab, har ehsaas mein…
Aur dheere dheere
Ek ajeeb sa raaz khulne laga.

Jise main **“tu”** samajhta tha
Wo shayad kabhi
“Mujh se alag” tha hi nahi.

Tu hawa thi
Jo meri saanson mein chalti rahi.

Tu leher thi
Jo mere andar ke sagar ko jagati rahi.

Tu ek roop thi
Usi ek astitva ka
Jise log kabhi ishq kehte hain
Kabhi khuda.

Aur aaj jab kabhi
Tera ehsaas hawa ki tarah
Mujh se takraata hai…

Main mitti ban kar
Usmein bikhar jaata hoon.

Na ismein dard hai
Na shikayat.

Bas ek ajeeb sa sukoon hai.

Jaise ek leher ko
Aakhir samajh aa gaya ho —

Wo kabhi sagar se alag thi hi nahi.

Aur tab dil ke kisi gehre kone se
Ek khamosh si awaaz uthti hai —

Na tu meri thi…
Na main tera tha.

Hum dono
Bas ek hi kahani ke
Do lafz the.

Aur jab kahani poori hui…

Dono wapas
Khamoshi mein sama gaye.

Aur phir:

Ye ending **Rumi / Osho / Advaita vibe** deti hai.

* **Rumi** → ishq ko khuda tak ka raasta
